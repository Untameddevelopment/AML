How to Use AlphaMetric Linguistics

            
1. Generate Mapping: Enter a seed (optional) and click "Generate Mapping" to get a unique character to number mapping.

            
2. Convert to Characters: Enter numbers (comma-separated) and click "Convert to Characters" to decode them into characters using the generated mapping.

            
3. Convert to Numbers: Enter characters and click "Convert to Numbers" to encode them into numbers using the generated mapping.

            
4. Secure Communication: Share the seed and mapping with someone to enable secure communication—only those with the same seed can understand the messages.

            
Feel free to experiment with different seeds to create unique mappings and explore the vast world of AlphaMetric Linguistics!
